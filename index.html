FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How Does it Work?

Search the app for products you want to win with approximate retail values of $50-$1,000 and spin for a chance to win. You can add up to 10 products to Win It! and spin once daily for each product. When you spin, you have the chance to win the product you want or Shop Your Way Rewards points. No purchase is necessary to enter or win. You will also have the opportunity to view ads and offers. Advertisers pay to display relevant ads based upon the product that was selected. We use the revenue we earn to purchase the products people win.

How many products can I add?

You can add up to 10 products to Win It! and spin once daily for each product. You can also remove products and add new ones.

I Won! Now what?

CONGRATS! Check your email for a confirmation of your win and important information about verifying your eligibility to win and receiving your prize. We will confirm your address and size or color options if applicable. If you won points, they will be credited to your Shop Your Way account automatically.
